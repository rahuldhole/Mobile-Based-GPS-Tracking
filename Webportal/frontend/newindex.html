Welcome to SGGS Bus Tracer App

This App is design for SGGS IE&T, Nanded only others are prohibited to use.


Switch on GPS and Data Pack from Setting >> Location >> Wireless/ Mobile Network/ A-GPS Enable it.
Click on Login tab below >> Select which bus you want to track >> Set auto update time >> and dont forgot to log out otherwise bus will be remains visible on map


What to do if Loacation is not updating


1. turn on and off and on your Data connection
2. turn on and off and on your GPS connection
3. turn on and off and on your Setting>>Location>> on-off-on services
4. Restart the App
5. Restart your Device
6. if still not happening then please report the error to the developers.
